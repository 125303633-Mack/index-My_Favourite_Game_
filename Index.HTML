My favourite Game

By: Conor Mackey
125303633@umail.ucc.ie

I've had one specific favourite game of mine for a very long time, ever since i was around the age of 9 or 10. That game is Halo 3.



™/®Bungie; Microsoft Game Studios

I spent an ungodly amount of time playing this game by myself, with my brothers / cousins / friends all over my estate growing up. I was introduced to it by one of my older brothers friends when he brought it over to our house. Up until then I had only ever really played nintendo games on the WII console. This was the first "grown up" game I ever got my hands on. I think its still as fun to play now as it was back then. It being my favourite is definitely influenced by a massive amount of nostalgia.
